This Privacy Policy explains how CerebroOS ("CerebroOS," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Trackspense, our expense-tracking application, available on the web at expense.cerebroos.com and as a mobile app (together, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by CerebroOS, located at CerebroOS HQ. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@cerebroos.com.

2. Information we collect

a. Information you provide

Category Examples Why we collect it
Account & profile Email address, name, phone number, and (optionally) mailing address To create and secure your account and provide the Service
Financial & expense data Expense descriptions, merchant/store names, amounts, dates, categories, recurring-payment templates, and income figures you enter Core functionality — to record, categorize, and analyze your spending
Receipts & uploads Receipt images you upload and the text extracted from them ("Parse Receipt" OCR) To let you capture expenses from receipts
AI queries Questions you type into the AI Analyst and the financial context needed to answer them To generate answers about your spending
Support & ideas Messages, feedback, and ideas you submit To respond to you and improve the Service

b. Information collected automatically

When you use the Service we automatically collect certain technical and usage data, including device and browser type, IP address, pages viewed, and interactions. We use Google Analytics for this. On the web, our infrastructure provider (Cloudflare) may also process request data to deliver and protect the Service.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to: provide, maintain, and improve the Service; record and analyze your expenses; generate insights, charts, and AI-assisted answers; process recurring-expense templates; authenticate you and secure your account; respond to support requests; monitor performance and prevent abuse; and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Artificial intelligence features

The AI Analyst answers questions about your finances. To do this, your query and the relevant financial context are sent to a third-party AI model provider (currently OpenAI) for processing. We send only the data needed to answer your request, and we ask that any such provider protect your data to a standard at least equivalent to this Policy. If you do not want your data processed by a third-party AI provider, do not use the AI Analyst feature. We will obtain your consent before sharing your data with AI services where required by applicable law or app-store rules.

5. How we share information

We share personal information only as follows:

  • Service providers. Vendors who process data on our behalf to run the Service, including hosting (Google Cloud), content delivery/security (Cloudflare), analytics (Google Analytics), and the AI model provider (OpenAI). They are permitted to use the data only to provide services to us and must protect it to a standard equivalent to this Policy.
  • Legal & safety. When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or CerebroOS.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share your financial data with advertisers.

6. Data retention

We retain your personal and financial information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account (see Section 8), we delete or de-identify your personal and financial data within 30 days, except where we must retain certain records to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you as required by law.

8. Your rights and choices

You can:

  • Access and update your profile and expense data within the Service.
  • Export your data by [EXPORT METHOD — e.g. "using the export option in Settings" or "emailing us"].
  • Delete your account and data from the profile screen ("Delete Account"). This permanently deletes your account and associated expense data and cannot be undone.
  • Revoke consent (for example, stop using the AI Analyst or opt out of analytics) at any time.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or the CCPA/CPRA — including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@cerebroos.com.

9. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children and is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International data transfers

We operate the Service using infrastructure located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where we or our providers operate, which may have different data-protection laws than your own.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you in the Service. Your continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact us

Questions or requests? Email privacy@cerebroos.com or write to CerebroOS, CerebroOS HQ.
